Our Services

Waterblasting 10k to 40k PSI

Powerful high-pressure waterblasting up to 40,000 PSI for tough industrial cleanup. Our confined-space trained team uses specialized attachments and controlled techniques to cut through buildup, clean surfaces, and prep equipment safely and efficiently. 

Roof Ballast Removal


RVI provides fast, efficient roof ballast removal using both Dry and Wet Vacuum Systems, handling everything from pea gravel to river rock while reducing dust, improving worker safety, and keeping projects in compliance. With over 20 years of experience, we deliver high-quality results on every job, including multi-story and glass-exterior buildings. For large roof projects requiring on-site or off-site disposal, our Shuttle Vac® system offers a cost-effective solution—contact us for a free quote and let our team handle your roof preparation needs from start to finish.

Wet & Dry Vacuuming 


Our Wet and Dry Vacuum Systems provide powerful, high-capacity cleanup for industrial sites, roof projects, and waste removal. The Wet Vacuum System deep-cleans surfaces so adhesives and roofing materials bond more effectively, while the Dry Vacuum System quickly removes ballast, reduces dust, improves worker safety, and keeps your project compliant. From pea gravel to river rock, our equipment delivers reliable, efficient results that create a cleaner and safer work environment.

Tank Cleaning & Tank Repair


RVI offers complete cleaning and repair services for fixed, mobile, and transport tanks, including railroad tankers, semi tankers, storage tanks, and production tanks. Our team safely handles everything from routine maintenance to full tank decommissioning when required. Equipped to manage tanks of any size or type, we deliver efficient, professional service—there’s no tank too big or too small for us.
